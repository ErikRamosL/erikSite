Projects

Space Shooter

Space Shooter is a shooter game where you play as a space marine defending his space station from the invaders. This game was developed with GameSalad.

Controls: LEFT ARROW & RIGHT ARROW keys to move. SPACE to shoot.

Fat Boys Adventure Through Time

Fat Boys Adventure Through Time is a 2D platformer where you play as joey, a boy trying to defend his city . This game was developed with GameSalad

Controls: LEFT ARROW & RIGHT ARROW keys to move. X to shoot and Z to jump.

Island Defence: Iwo Jima

Island Defence: Iwo Jima is a top-down shooter where you play as "Gatz" who was sent to the island Iwo Jima with his platoon to defend the island, but his platoon was killed and now he is the only one left to defend the island from the japanese. This game was developed with GameSalad

Controls: W, A, S, & D to move around, R to reload, F to switch weapon.
